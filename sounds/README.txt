Sound Files for Chicken or Not Game

This folder should contain the following sound files:

1. correct.mp3 - A happy/success sound effect to play when the player makes a correct guess.
   Recommended: A cheerful chicken cluck or a victory jingle.

2. wrong.mp3 - A funny/failure sound effect to play when the player makes an incorrect guess.
   Recommended: A comical "wrong answer" sound or a disappointed chicken noise.

Please add your own sound files with these names to make the game fully functional.
